# Quick Start: Voice Test Feature

## What's New?

Your Cogniscope application now supports **voice-based cognitive assessments**! Users can answer certain questions by speaking rather than selecting multiple-choice options.

## How It Works

### For Users

1. **Start a Test**
   - Go to your dashboard and click "Start New Test"
2. **Answer Voice Questions**
   - When you see a voice question, you'll see a recording interface
   - Click **"Start Recording"** and grant microphone permission when prompted
   - Speak your answer clearly into your microphone
   - Click **"Stop"** when you're done
   - Use **"Pause"** if you need to think during recording
3. **Review Your Answer**
   - Listen to your recording using the playback controls
   - If not satisfied, click **"Record Again"** to re-record
4. **Submit and Continue**
   - Click **"Submit Answer"** to save your response
   - Move to the next question

### Example Voice Questions

You might encounter questions like:

- "Describe what you did this morning"
- "Name as many animals as you can think of"
- "Explain what the room you're in looks like"
- "Tell us what a proverb means"

## Features

✅ **Easy Recording** - One-click recording with pause/resume  
✅ **Playback** - Review your answer before submitting  
✅ **Re-record** - Not happy? Record again!  
✅ **Timer** - See how long you've been recording  
✅ **Visual Feedback** - Pulsing indicator shows active recording

## Requirements

- **Microphone** - Built-in or external mic
- **Modern Browser** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- **Microphone Permission** - You'll be asked to grant access
- **HTTPS Connection** - For security (automatically handled in production)

## Troubleshooting

### "Microphone Access Denied"

- Click the 🔒 icon in your browser's address bar
- Allow microphone access for this site
- Refresh the page

### "No Sound Recorded"

- Check that your microphone is connected
- Verify it's not muted
- Try speaking louder and closer to the mic
- Test your microphone in another application

### "Can't Hear Playback"

- Check your device volume
- Make sure your speakers/headphones are connected
- Try a different browser

## Privacy & Security

- ✅ Your voice recordings are encrypted during transmission
- ✅ Audio data is only accessible by you and authorized clinicians
- ✅ Recordings are used solely for cognitive assessment
- ✅ You must grant explicit permission to use your microphone

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Find a Quiet Space** - Reduce background noise
2. **Speak Clearly** - Natural pace, clear pronunciation
3. **Stay Close to Mic** - But not too close (6-12 inches is ideal)
4. **Take Your Time** - Think before you speak
5. **Be Natural** - Answer in your own words

## Need Help?

If you experience any issues:

1. Try refreshing the page
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your microphone settings
4. Contact support if problems persist
